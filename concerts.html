On Sunday 26 September 2021, we played a full concert at Prince of Peace Lutheran Church in Dublin, OH. We had the honor of kicking off their '21-22 concert series, and we had a great time performing in such beautiful looking and sounding space! You can watch the full concert on YouTube here.

On Saturday 28 August 2021 at 1pm, we played another full concert at Stanton's Sheet Music. This time we played some new music, and we had guest cellist, Isaiah Wu, playing along with us for part of the program!

We played during Sunday morning worship services at Worthington United Methodist Church on 15 August 2021. We were so glad to share our music with the congregation there, and we couldn't have asked for better weather during the outdoor service! Maybe just a little less wind..

On 24 July 2021 we played an outdoor show accompanied by beautiful weather at Wolfe Park by Bexley, OH. The performance was part of a series called 'Park Pop-Ups' by the Greater Columbus Arts Council and the Columbus Recreation and Parks Department.

We played our first full indoor concert on 17 July 2021 at Stanton's. We are so thankful to those of you who came to share the moment with us!
